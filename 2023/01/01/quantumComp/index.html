∣ψ⟩=eiγ(cos⁡θ2∣0⟩+sin⁡θ2eiφ∣1⟩)\ket{\psi}=e^{i\gamma}(\cos\frac{\theta}{2}\ket{0}+\sin\frac{\theta}{2}e^{i\varphi}\ket{1}) ∣ψ⟩=eiγ(cos2θ​∣0⟩+sin2θ​eiφ∣1⟩)
